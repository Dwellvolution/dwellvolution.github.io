TERMITE BAITS

HOW DOES SENTRICON WORKS?

 Sentricon is a baiting system which is installed into the ground around the building. It has cellulose baits impregnated with active ingredient _____________ which attracts termites, and ingest it then passes it to the entire colony resulting to terminate entire infestation. Colony elimination is the Most effective way in protecting your property. 

The bait's active ingredient has a growth inhibitor which when takes over it prevents the termites from maturing through molting, then they die off.

Sentricon is an eco-friendly product and a leading alternative to a liquid termiticide. 

COST OF SENTRICON TERMITE TREATMENT

Cost for Sentricon termite management may vary depending on the size of the property. Cost is calculated per lineal meter.

HOW LONG DOES THE SENTRICON TAKES PLACE?

The installation of Sentricon system will depend on the size of the property to be protected. 

However, Sentricon system begins working once termites feed on the bait found within the station. If termites are present, they will find the stations. If active termite feeding is visible, a pest professional will install above-ground stations to initiate immediate feeding. The faster worker termites get to the bait used by the Sentricon system, the faster their colony will be eliminated. Colony elimination is the most effective way to protect your property.
